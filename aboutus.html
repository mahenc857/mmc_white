About Us

Mahendra Metal Corporation, an ISO 9001:2015 certified company based in Mumbai, was established in 1973 with the objective of meeting the growing demand for Stainless Steel and Nickel Alloys. Over the decades, we have emerged as a trusted and leading supplier of high-quality stainless steel and nickel alloy products.

Driven by a team of young visionaries, we continuously strive for excellence across every aspect of our business. Our foundation is built on strong core values, ethical business practices, and a relentless pursuit of customer satisfaction.

With a customer-centric approach, timely deliveries, and uncompromised quality, Mahendra Metal Corporation has expanded its product range and market presence. Our long-standing relationships with customers across India reflect the trust and confidence we have earned in the industry.

With the new generation of entrepreneurs joining the organization, we aim to scale globally by strengthening both domestic supply and exports of Stainless Steel and Nickel Alloy Sheets, Plates, and Coils.

Our goal is to offer Stainless Steel and Nickel Alloy Sheets, Plates, and Coils under one roof, ensuring superior quality materials that precisely meet customer requirements.

In addition to standard sizes, we specialize in providing customized solutions based on customer drawings and samples, delivering products tailored exactly to specific applications.

We conduct comprehensive testing including Chemical, Physical, Mechanical, Ultrasonic, Micro, and IGC tests through Government and internationally approved certification bodies. Quality remains our highest priority.
